# Footnote — brand

**Name:** Footnote — a collaborative review + light editing layer for native-LaTeX documents
("Google Docs for LaTeX"). Working name; not yet wired into the app UI.

**Mark:** `footnote-mark.svg` — a margin rule with lines of text and a comment dot in the margin. The
margin rule + two text lines also read as an **F**. Reuses the review-portal accent blue.

**Palette (matches the app + emails):**
- Accent blue `#2c64c4` (tile fill)
- White `#ffffff` (mark strokes/dot)
- On a dark bar, tile stays blue; wordmark white.

**Usage:**
- App favicon / top-bar mark: use `footnote-mark.svg` at 20–34px.
- Wordmark: "Footnote", weight 600, letter-spacing -0.5px, in `--text` / white on dark.
- Keep the tile's rounded corners (rx≈12 on a 52 viewBox ≈ 23%).

**Not yet done:** wiring the mark/wordmark into the reviewer top bars, favicon, and page titles (deferred
until the name is confirmed for rollout).
